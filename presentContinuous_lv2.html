English Grammar Test - Present Continuous LV2

긍정형 (Affirmative Sentences)

1. She ____ dinner right now.
그녀는 지금 저녁을 준비하고 있다.
a) is preparing
b) is eating
c) is informing
2. The children ____ their skills in the park.
아이들은 공원에서 기술을 발달시키고 있다.
a) are playing
b) are developing
c) are hiding
3. I ____ excellent service this week.
나는 이번 주에 훌륭한 서비스를 받고 있다.
a) am receiving
b) am giving
c) am ignoring
4. They ____ the audience at the moment.
그들은 지금 청중에게 알리고 있다.
a) are entertaining
b) are informing
c) are confusing
5. John ____ his relationship with his friend in his room.
존은 방에서 친구와 자신의 관계를 논의하고 있다.
a) is discussing
b) is ending
c) is avoiding

부정형 (Negative Sentences)

6. She ____ her project right now.

b) is not starting
c) is not finishing

a) is not inviting
b) is not paying
c) is not requiring
8. We ____ for the event next week.
우리는 다음 주 행사에 자원봉사를 하지 않을 것이다.
a) are not preparing
b) are not volunteering
c) are not attending
9. He ____ well in school today.
그는 오늘 학교에서 바르게 행동하지 않고 있다.
a) is not behaving
b) is not studying
c) is not listening
10. My parents ____ enough opportunities for me.
부모님은 나에게 충분한 기회를 제공하지 않고 있다.
a) are not advising
b) are not providing
c) are not expecting
